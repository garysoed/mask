Colors in Mask start with two seed colors: "base" color and "accent" color.

Based on the seeds, Mask will generate three palettes:

You can also specify the context of a theme using the mk-theme-context attribute. There are three contexts supported:

  1. main: This is the main content of the page.
  2. side: This is a side content, such as headers, footers, and sidebars.
  3. highlight: These are highlighted contents. Examples include banners and tooltips.
