Colors in Mask start with two seed colors: "base" color and "accent" color.

Base seed
Accent seed

Based on the seeds, Mask will generate three palettes:

  1. Passive (P): The most commonly used palette. This is used for any non interactable components.
  2. Action (A): Used for any actionable elements.
  3. Highlight (H): Used for feedback for player's actions, such as hover and focus effects.
